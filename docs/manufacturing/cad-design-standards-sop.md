# CAD Design Standards SOP

**Status: PLACEHOLDER — to be written by the owner.** This is the standard a CAD QC peer reviewer checks a
submitted STL against before approving it to production (see `customs-workflow.md` §3 step 6). For now it is a
free-form document; later it may become a structured checklist surfaced in the QC review UI.

The QC reviewer (a CAD designer other than the author) earns a flat **QC review fee**
(`adminSettings.financial.qcReviewFee`) for performing this review; the fee is folded into the custom's COGS.
